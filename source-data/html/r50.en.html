Printable PDF 6 cucharadas de mantequilla; 2 cucharaditas de sal; 1-4 cucharadita de pimienta; 3 cucharadas de harina de tri go; 6 tazas de leche caliente; 1 cucharada de cebolla rallada; 2 tazas de papas majadas 6 tablespoons butter; 2 teaspoons salt; 1-4 teaspoon pepper; 3 tablespoons tri flour go; 6 cups of hot milk; 1 tablespoon grated onion; 2 cups mashed potatoes
  1. Prepare the bechamel by melting the butter.
  2. Add salt, pepper and flour and stir until it forms a pass.
  3. Pour the hot milk and onion and move constantly.
  4. When it boils add the mashed potatoes.
  5. Serve hot.
