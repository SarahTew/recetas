Printable PDF 1 libra de bacalao; 1 cebolla rebanada; aceitunas a gusto; 2 tomates rebanados; salsa francesa 1 pound of cod; 1 sliced onion; olives to taste; 2 sliced tomatoes; French sauce
  1. Put the cod in water for a few hours to remove some of the salt
  2. Boil, remove the skin and thorns and cut into small pieces.
  3. Place the cod in a bowl and garnish with tomato wheels, onions and olives.
  4. Pour the French sauce.
  5. Serve with other boiled foods: banana, sweet potato, potatoes, etc.
