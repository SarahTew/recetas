Printable PDF 1 libra de habichuelas; 2 chorizos desmenuzados; 3 huevos batidos; 1 cucharadita de cebolla rallada; 1-4 cucharadita de pimienta; manteca 1 pound of beans; 2 crumbled chorizos; 3 beaten eggs; 1 teaspoon grated onion; 1-4 teaspoon pepper; butter
  1. Soften the beans and mash them.
  2. Add the other ingredients and mix.
  3. Put enough butter in the pan to cover the bottom.
  4. Pour into the pan, cover and simmer, come back and brown on both sides.
