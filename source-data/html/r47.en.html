Printable PDF 2 paquetes de gelatina con sabor de naranja; 2 tazas de agua hirviendo; 1 3-4 taza de jugo de naranja; fruta fresca 2 packets of orange-flavored gelatin; 2 cups boiling water; 1 3-4 cup orange juice; fresh fruit
  1. Dissolve the gelatin in the boiling water.
  2. Add the orange juice.
  3. Pour into a 1 liter capacity thread mold and cool until firm.
  4. Remove from the pan and pour into a serving plate.
  5. In the center of the mold, place fresh fruit.
