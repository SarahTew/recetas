Printable PDF 2 tazas de arroz; sal gruesa; 5 tazas de agua caliente; 1 hoja de laurel; 1 cascarilla de limón; jugo de limón; 6 higados de ave; manteca; 1-2 cebolla en juliana fina; 1 vaso de vino marsala; 1 pizca de sal; 1 pizca de pimienta negra; 1 pizca de canela; 2 cucharaditas de fécula de arroz; 1 taza de leche tibia 2 cups rice; coarse salt; 5 cups of hot water; 1 bay leaf; 1 lemon husk; lemon juice; 6 poultry livers; lard; 1-2 onion in fine julienne; 1 glass of marsala wine; 1 pinch of salt; 1 pinch of black pepper; 1 pinch of cinnamon; 2 teaspoons rice starch; 1 cup warm milk
  1. Put in a clay pot the rice with the salt, water, bay leaf, a piece of lemon peel and a little lemon juice.
  2. Let the uncovered rice cook for 15 minutes.
  3. Remove it from the heat and leave it warm inside the oven, semi-open to dry and open the grain.Save it.
  4. Apart, in a pan, put plenty of butter and fry the poultry livers in it, clean and washed in milk.
  5. When they begin to brown, add the onion, wine and seasonings
  6. Slide the rice starch into the milk and thicken it to lightly epesar the sauce.
  7. Stir gently to mix everything well.
  8. This preparation should be made at medium temperature so that the livers do not harden.
  9. Present in a large dish, placing the liver sauce in the center and the rice as a border.
