Printable PDF 6 tomates; 1 cebolla; 3 berenjenas; 1 ajo; 1 taza de vino blanco; 3 yemas de huevo; perejil; 1 taza queso rallado; polvo de galletas; sal 6 tomatoes; 1 onion; 3 eggplants; 1 garlic; 1 cup white wine; 3 egg yolks; parsley; 1 cup grated cheese; cookie powder; salt
  1. Tomatoes are divided into two and are emptied, seasoned with salt and pepper.
  2. They are fried with the onion cut into small pieces the eggplant in thin slices.
  3. They are sprinkled with white wine.
  4. Add the salt to taste, parsley and grated cheese.
  5. Let it cool and add the three egg yolks
  6. Tomatoes are stuffed and sprinkled with cookie powder
  7. They are placed in a mold, sprinkled with grated Parmesan cheese and au gratin in the oven for 5 minutes.
  8. They are served in the same juice.
