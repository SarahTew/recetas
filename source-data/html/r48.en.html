Printable PDF 6 tazas de papas cocidas y cortadas en dados; 1 1/2 taza de apio picado; 6 cucharadas de pimiento, picado; 3/4 taza de Mayonesa Kraft; 1 cucharada de sal de cebolla o ajo si prefiere; pimienta recién molida; lechuga; huevos duros; 7 rebanadas de salchichón; 6 cucharadas de aderezo de tomate 6 cups cooked potatoes and diced; 1 1/2 cup chopped celery; 6 tablespoons pepper, chopped; 3/4 cup Kraft Mayonnaise; 1 tablespoon of onion or garlic salt if you prefer; freshly ground pepper; lettuce; hard-boiled eggs; 7 slices of sausage; 6 tablespoons tomato dressing
  1. Stir the potatoes, celery and pepper with the mayonnaise.
  2. Salt and pepper are added to taste.
  3. Put a mound of salad on arranged lettuce on a plate.
  4. Garnish the salad with lettuce and shell-shaped eggs are arranged.
  5. Arrange slices of sausage in the form of wafers, fill these with tomato dressing and place them around the salad.
