Printable PDF 1 seso grande; 200 gramas de harina; 2 yemas; leche 1 large brain; 200 grams of flour; 2 egg yolk; milk
  1. Prepare a large brain, removing the skin and cooking it in water and salt
  2. drain it and cut it into pieces.
  3. Put in a bowl the flour, yolks and milk in sufficient quantity to form a soft preparation
  4. mix well, add the brains, fry them by tablespoons in hot oil and over moderate heat.
