Printable PDF 2 tazas de dulce de coco; 2 cucharadas de mantequilla derretida; 2 huevos batidos; 2 onzas de almendras pelada 2 cups dulce de coco; 2 tablespoons melted butter; 2 beaten eggs; 2 ounces peeled almonds
  1. Add the butter and eggs to the dulce de coco.
  2. Pour into a flat greased mold.
  3. Garnish with almonds.
  4. Bake over moderate heat for 20 minutes.
