Printable PDF 2 libras de papas; 1 cucharadita de sal; 2 huevos batidos; 4 cucharadas de harina; 2 tazas de picadillo de carne para relleno 2 pounds of potatoes; 1 teaspoon salt; 2 beaten eggs; 4 tablespoons flour; 2 cups minced meat filling
  1. Boil and mash the potatoes.
  2. Add salt, eggs, and flour.
  3. Divide into two portions.
  4. Put a part in a greased mold so that it shakes the entire mold.
  5. Pour the picadillo and spread it and rub with the rest of the potatoes.
  6. Cook in a moderate oven at 350 degrees F for 30 or 40 minutes.
