• 1 1-2 cups of packed cold coffee
  • 1 to 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1-2 teaspoon vanilla.
  1. Fill the bowl of the electric mixer halfway with well-chopped ice.
  2. Then add 1 1 -2 cups of packed cold coffee, 1 to 2 tablespoons of sugar and 1-2 teaspoons of vanilla.
  3. Mix for about 2 or 3 minutes.
  4. Makes 4 glasses of a rich, frothy coffee drink.
