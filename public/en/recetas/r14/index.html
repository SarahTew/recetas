baked plantains

Recipe in Chronicling America |

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe plantains
  • 1/2 pound of cheese broken into long slices
  • butter

Directions

  1. Peel the plantains.
  2. Once peeled cut down center.
  3. Bake, after filling with cheese, in a mold with butter.
  4. Once golden, prepare a white syrup and serve.
