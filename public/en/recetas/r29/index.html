orange cakes

Recipe in Chronicling America
  • 300 grams of flour
  • 150 grams of sugar
  • 2 teaspoon baking powder
  • half a teaspoon of salt
  • half a teaspoon of baking soda
  • 100 grams of butter
  • 1 orange
  1. Pass through a sifter 300 grams of flour, 150 of sugar, 2 teaspoons filled with baking powder, half salt and a half baking soda.
  2. Mix a cup of orange juice with the zest of an orange, 100 grams of barely melted butter, a beaten egg and add it to the other ingredients
  3. put in a buttered and floured mold do and cook like the previous ones.
  4. You can make a bath with 100 grams of butter, 200 of impalpable sugar, juice and zest of that fruit.
