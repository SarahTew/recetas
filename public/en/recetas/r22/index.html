breaded meat medallions

Recipe in Chronicling America

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of ground beef
  • 1 grated onion
  • 1/2 tablespoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cup cracker meal
  • oil
  • 8 toasted bread medallions
  • mustard sauce

Directions

  1. Place the meat in a bowl and add the onion, salt, pepper and nutmeg. Mix well.
  2. Add 2 eggs, mixing each one in well.
  3. Add cracker meal to give consistency to the dough, but it should still be soft.
  4. Divide into 8 parts.
  5. Each part is bowled and crushed until it is 1/4 inch of thickness.
  6. Pass each part through breadcrumbs or cracker meal, beaten eggs and crumbs again.
  7. Fry in hot oil, until golden brown.
  8. Sauce is poured on them.
  9. They are served hot. If desired they are garnished with lemon wheels powdered with paprika.
