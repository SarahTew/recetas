Italian rice

Recipe in Chronicling America

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound chopped onions
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 4 ounces of broken ham
  • 3 split tomatoes
  • 3 split anchovies
  • 1 1/2 pounds of rice
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 1 minced chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Sauté and brown the onions and add the ham, tomatoes, anchovies.
  2. Add the rice, stir and sauté.
  3. Add the broth and chicken.
  4. When the rice is almost dry, add the cheese, stir, and pour into a grease mold. Sprinkle with ground cracker.
  5. Bake in a moderate heat oven for 15 minutes, or cook on the stove with the lid on.
