liver fillets

Recipe in Chronicling America

Ingredients

  • liver fillets
  • flour
  • salt
  • oil
  • a bay leaf, 50 grams of butter
  • a glass of Marsala wine
  • cooked spinach
  • lard
  • cooked potatoes

Directions

  1. Cut some liver fillets by tucking the tails and nerves and pass them through flour and season with salt.
  2. Put in a frying pan half a cup of oil, heat well and brown the encores
  3. then add a bay leaf, 50 grams of butter, a glass of Marsala wine, cover and let it boil slowly until the fillets are cooked and the sauce reduced.
  4. Serve them accompanied with cooked spinach and drizzled with butter and cooked potatoes.
