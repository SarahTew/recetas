grilled lobster

Recipe in Chronicling America |

Ingredients

  • 1 3-pound lobster
  • butter
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Cut the lobster lengthwise without removing the shell.
  2. Remove the stomach and intestines.
  3. Place each half on the grill, resting on the shell.
  4. Spread butter and sprinkle with pepper to taste.
  5. Simmer for 40 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with melted butter.
