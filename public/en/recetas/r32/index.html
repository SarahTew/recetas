panetela portena

Recipe in Chronicling America |

Ingredients

  • 1 panetela
  • 1 glass of rum
  • 8 tablespoons grated coconut do
  • 10 candied cherries
  • 1 kilogram of dulce de leche

Directions

  1. The panetela is cut into layers fine and sprinkled with rum, covering with dulce de leche.
  2. It is formed again and covered above and around with the same sweet.
  3. It sticks striped coconut around and is decorated on top with the rest of the sweet.
  4. Garnish with candied cherries split in half.
