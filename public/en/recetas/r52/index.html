creamy pumpkin soup

Recipe in Chronicling America |

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of mashed pumpkin
  • 3 cups bechamel sauce
  • 1 small onion, grated

Directions

  1. Add onion and bechemel sauce to mashed pumpkin.
  2. Bring to a boil and season at ease.
  3. Strain and or broth.
  4. Serve callente with crackers or toasted bread.
