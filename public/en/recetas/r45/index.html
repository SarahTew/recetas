corncakes

Recipe in Chronicling America
  • 2 cups corn flour
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 pound of pork
  • 1 onion
  • 2 ounces of ham
  • 3 tablespoons lard
  • 1 pepper
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • lard
  1. Prepare a hard funche using flour, water and salt.
  2. Grind the other ingredients into the machine and add the salt.
  3. Simmer.
  4. Season at ease.
  5. On a greased banana leaf, pour two tablespoons of funche and spread it in a circle.
  6. Put a tablespoon of filling in the center and double in two affirming the edges so that they come together.
  7. Fry in hot lard and drain on absorbent paper.
