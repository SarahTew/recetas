corncakes

Recipe in Chronicling America
Printable Recipe (PDF)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cornmeal
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 pound of pork
  • 1 onion
  • 2 ounces of ham
  • 3 tablespoons oil
  • 1 pepper
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • lard

Directions

  1. Prepare a thick polenta using cornmeal, water and salt.
  2. Grind the other ingredients and add the salt.
  3. Simmer. Season to taste.
  4. On a greased banana leaf, pour two tablespoons of the polenta and spread it in a circle.
  5. Add a tablespoon of filling in the center and fold in two affirming the edges so that they come together.
  6. Fry in hot oil and drain on absorbent paper.
