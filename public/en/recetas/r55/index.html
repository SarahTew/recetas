gypsy eggs

Recipe in Chronicling America

Ingredients

  • regular amount of oil
  • two garlic
  • 10 or 12 peeled almonds
  • a little piece of bread
  • a few strands of saffron 1/2 teaspoons of cumin
  • a little cinnamon powder
  • boiling water
  • salt
  • 7 eggs

Directions

  1. In a regular amount of oil we will start by frying two garlic, 10 or 12 peeled almonds, a piece of bread, removing when it is browned.
  2. In an almirez you start by crushing a few strands of saffron 1/2 teaspoon of cumin, a little cinnamon powder
  3. then the garlic, almonds and bread, soaking with drops of the same oil.
  4. When it begins to transform into an ifna paste, add the rest of the oil with which we have fried the almonds, slide until you are a thick sauce, with boiling water, salt, etc.
  5. This sauce is poured into a dish that can be put in the oven, 7 eggs are broken and curdled in the oven
  6. serve with the soft yolk.
  7. They can be decorated with fried bread put in the edge of the fountain.
