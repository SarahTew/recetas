bean omelette

Recipe in Chronicling America
Printable Recipe (PDF)

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of beans
  • 2 crumbled chorizos
  • 3 beaten eggs
  • 1 teaspoon grated onion
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • butter

Directions

  1. Soften the beans and mash them.
  2. Add the other ingredients and mix.
  3. Put enough butter in the pan to cover the bottom.
  4. Pour into the pan, cover and simmer. Make sure to brown on both sides.
