Ingredients

  • 1 4-pound chicken
  • 3 teaspoons salt
  • 3 cloves of crushed garlic
  • half a teaspoon of oregano powder
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1-1-2 pounds of pork
  • a quarter pound of ham
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon raisins

Directions

  1. Marinate the chicken inside and outside several hours before filling it.
  2. Grind the ham and the pork and add the other ingredients seasoning and re llane the chicken.
  3. Thing the opening and grease it on the outside with butter.
  4. Cook in the oven in an uncovered mold and when golden cover with the lid and simmer for 2 hours.
