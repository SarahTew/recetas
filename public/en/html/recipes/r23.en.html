Ingredients

  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup of oil
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 cup of tomato sauce
  • 1 can of tomatoes
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground pepper

Directions

  1. Fry the onion in the oil.
  2. Add the other ingredients and simmer until it thickens, approximately half an hour.
  3. Mash and strain.
