• 1 brain
  • 2 beaten eggs
  • 4 tablespoons of breadcrumbs
  • 6 tablespoons grated cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1-8 teaspoon pepper
  1. Boil the brain, mash and strain through a cheesecloth.
  2. Add the eggs, bread, cheese, butter, salt and pepper.
  3. Season it to taste and pour it into a greased mold.
  4. Bake at a temperature of 350 degrees F for 30 minutes.
