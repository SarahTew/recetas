Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds steak meat (beefsteak)
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon pepper black powder
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon cider vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon powders chili
  • 1 teaspoon split oregano leaves
  • 2 8-ounce cans of tomato sauce

Directions

  1. Cut the meat into 4 servings.
  2. Mash the garlic and mix with the salt and pepper.
  3. Adobe the meat with this everywhere.
  4. Combine lemon juice and vinegar, chili powders and oregano.
  5. Pour over the meat and leave it soaked for three hours.
  6. Then put it in a mold and sprinkle the tomato sauce.
  7. Roast for 15 minutes or until browned on one side, then dress it on the other, and leave it for another 15 minutes, or until it is completely brown and tender.
  8. For 4 servings.
