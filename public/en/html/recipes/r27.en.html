Ingredients

  • 2 bundles of chards
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 1/2 cup of oil
  • 1/4 cup of vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of pepper

Directions

  1. Wash and boil the chards, for no more than 5 minutes, in pieces and mix in the oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  2. Garnish with rings of onions and tomato.
