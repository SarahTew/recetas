Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound chopped onions
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 4 ounces of broken ham
  • 3 split tomatoes
  • 3 split anchovies
  • 1 1/2 pounds of rice
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 1 minced chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Brown the onions and add the ham, tomatoes, anchovies, and sauté.
  2. Add the rice, stir and sauté.
  3. Add the broth and chicken.
  4. When the rice is almost dry, add the cheese, stir, pour into a grease mold and sprinkle with ground cracker.
  5. Put in a moderate heat oven for 15 minutes, or on the grilled stove on the lid.
