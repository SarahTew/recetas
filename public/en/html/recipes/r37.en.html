Ingredients

  • 1 cup chickpeas
  • 1 cup peas
  • 3 red chorizos
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 2 bay leaflets
  • 1 sprig of parsley
  • 1 onion
  • 1 pepper
  • oil
  • salt

Directions

  1. Chickpeas and peas are left to soak from the day before, after washing them well.
  2. They are brought to a boil in the same water in which they have dawned.
  3. When they take point, the bay leaf, oregano, parsley are added, the onion and peppers are chopped and saut√©ed in oil with a pinch of salt and this is added along with the chorizos in pieces.
  4. It is stirred, allowed to reduce the sauce well and served very hot.
