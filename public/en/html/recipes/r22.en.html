Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of ground beef
  • 1 grated onion
  • 1/2 tablespoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cup cookie powder
  • frying fat
  • 8 toasted bread medallions
  • mustard sauce

Directions

  1. Place the meat in a bowl and add the onion, salt, pepper and nutmeg.
  2. Integrate well
  3. add 2 eggs, integrating each one ligating well.
  4. Add cracker meal to give consistency to the dough, but it should still be soft.
  5. Divide into 8 parts.
  6. Each part is bolea and crushed until it is left of Vi inch of thickness.
  7. They are passed through breadcrumbs or powdered biscuit, beaten eggs and biscuit again.
  8. Fried in fat hot, until golden brown.
  9. They slip away.
  10. They are placed on the bread medallion and the sauce is poured on them.
  11. They are served hot.
  12. If desired they are garnished with lemon wheels powdered with paprika.
