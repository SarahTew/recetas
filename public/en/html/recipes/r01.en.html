Ingredients

  • 4 chayotes
  • 4 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons raisins
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1-2 cup cracker meal

Directions

  1. Split the chayotes lengthwise and boil.
  2. When they are soft take out the pulp and mash well.
  3. Beat the yolks with the sugar, add salt, and cornstarch, milk, mashed chayotes, butter and potatoes
  4. Put on the fire and cook until thickened like a cream
  5. Fill the rinds with the cream and sprinkle cinnamon on top.
  6. Bake for ten minutes.
