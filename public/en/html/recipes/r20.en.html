• 1 pound of macaroni
  • 8 cups of water
  • 2 tablespoons salt
  • 1-4 pound of broken ham
  • 1-2 cup oil
  • 2 split onions
  • 1 clove of crushed garlic
  • 1-4 pound of oversized des menuzada
  • 1 can of tomato paste
  • 1 cup water
  1. Boil the macaroni in salted water and drain them after cooking.
  2. Fry the ham in the oil and add the other ingredients
  3. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Add the macaroni and stir.
  5. Serve hot.
