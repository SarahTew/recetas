Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound macaroni
  • 1/2 pounds of grated American cheese
  • 2 cups white sauce
  • 2 cans of Vienna sausages

Directions

  1. Cook the macaroni in plenty of salt water, until soft.
  2. Remove from heat, drain them and pour warm water again.
  3. Drain again.
  4. Make a white sauce with the additional ecipe we offered, and add the grated cheese.
  5. Cut into wheels several sausages, leaving 6 whole for adornment.
  6. Combine the macaroni, sauce, and sausage slices.
  7. Pour this mixture into a greased, square or round mold, as you prefer, but that is made of fireproof glass.
  8. Bake it at a temperature of 375 degrees F for 20 minutes.
  9. Take it out, place the slachichas in the center, as illustrated by the foel centro, with some parsley leaves as an ornament
  10. return the pan to the oven and have it cooking for 10 more minutes.
  11. Quantity for six people.
