  • sofrito
  • 5 split sausages
  • 1 1-2 pounds of rice
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons butter with annotto
  • 3 cups water
  1. Add the sausages, rice and butter with sofrito and cook for two minutes.
  2. Add the water and season to taste.
  3. Cook until the rice is a little dry, lower the heat, stir two or three times and keep covered until serving.
