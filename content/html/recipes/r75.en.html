Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 200 grams of flour
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 5 eggs
  • a can of tuna
  • mayonnaise
  • 150 grams of chopped and cooked ham
  • chopped lettuce
  • 3 bell peppers
  • chopped egg
  • chopped parsley

Directions

  1. Beat 6 eggs and 2 tablespoons of sugar, warm in a bain-marie, remove and beat until thickened.
  2. Slowly add 200 grams of flour, stirring salt and pepper
  3. Pour into greased and flouered pan
  4. Bake 50 minutes in moderate oven and remove from mold.
  5. Prepare mayonnaise from 5 eggs.
  6. Open a can of tuna and mix with a little mayonnaise.
  7. Cut the now cold biscuit into 4 layers.
  8. In the first put mayonnaise and 150 grams of cooked, chopped ham.
  9. in the second chopped lettuce and 3 bell peppers.
  10. and in the third put the tuna with mayonnaise,.
  11. Cover with the fourth layer.
  12. Cover everything with mayonnaise, scatter chopped egg and decorate the top with chopped parsley, chopped bell peppers and egg.
  13. Serve on a bed of lettuce.
