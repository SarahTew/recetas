Ingredients

  • 2 ounces of chocolate
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 teaspoons corn sirup
  • A quarter teaspoon of salt
  • 1 and a quarter cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions

  1. Grate and melt the chocolate, add the sugar, salt, butter, corn syrup and milk.
  2. Boil until it thickens a little and forms a soft ball by pouring a few drops into cold water.
  3. Remove from heat and let cool a little.
  4. Add the vanilla and beat continuously until it begins to harden
  5. Pour into a greased plate and cut into squares.
