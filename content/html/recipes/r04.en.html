Ingredients

  • 6 cups broth
  • 1/2 pound split pumpkin in pieces
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Directions

  1. Boil the pumpkin in the broth.
  2. Strain and mash the pumpkin, mix the flour with a little cold broth, add the butter and put on the fire, stirring constantly and add the rest of the broth.
  3. Season to taste and serve warm.
