Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup butter
  • 1 1/3 cups powdered suar
  • 3 egg yolks beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • I- teaspoon of salt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 11/2- teaspoons of cloves
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons walnut Flyme
  • 3/4 cups milk
  • 1 cup raisins
  • 3 egg whites beaten

Directions

  1. Soften butter.
  2. Add the sugar little by little and beat for a while
  3. Add the whipped yolks and vanilla and mix well
  4. Sifting together three times flour, baking powder salt, cinnamon, cloves, and nutmeg, and add to butter alternating with milk.
  5. Add raisins and egg whites
  6. Pour into a greased pan and cook in a moderate oven.
