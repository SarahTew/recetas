Ingredients

  • 3-4 pounds of ground beef
  • 3-4 pounds of ground pork
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1-2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1-4 teaspoon pepper powder
  • 1 egg
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1-4 cup cognac
  • cracker meal
  • 1-4 pound precooked ham cut into thin slices

Directions

  1. Place the meats in a bowl and add the salt, nutmeg and pepper.
  2. Stir until the ingredients are well mixed.
  3. The egg is added and when it is well bound, a yolk is added and after well-bound the second one is added.
  4. Cognac is added.
  5. It binds well.
  6. A powdered cookie is added until a mixture can be shaped.
  7. Spread on a waxed paper to form a rectangle of 12 x 8 inches.
  8. The ham flakes are placed on the meat.
  9. It rolls up in the narrowest part as if it were a gypsy arm.
  10. It is wrapped in a fine canvas previously moistened.
  11. The ends are tied well and the fabric joint is sewn.
  12. It is cooked in water hiriviendo to cover for 1 1-4 hours.
  13. To the cooking water is added an onion, a bay leaf, 4 peppercorns and two cloves.
  14. A tablespoon of salt is also added.
  15. After the cooking time, it is drained and allowed to cool to room temperature.
  16. It is cooled in the refrigerator and then the fabric is removed and served cut into thin wheels.
  17. If desired, it is accompanied with prunes.
