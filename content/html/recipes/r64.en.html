Ingredients

  • 9 eggs
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • a teaspoon of mustard
  • half a teaspoon of chopped parsley
  • 100 grams of marrow

Directions

  1. In a saucepan put a liter of water on the stove and when it starts to boil put in 9 eggs are put and leave to cook exactly 12 minutes.
  2. After this time, remove the saucepan from the stove, remove the eggs and soak them in cold water and remove shells
  3. Wash the eggs with cold water, split in half lengthwise and remove the yolk, leaving the egg whites whole.
  4. To the yolks add 3 tablespoons of milk, 5 grams of fine salt, a teaspoon of mustard, half a tablespoon of chopped parsley, and 100 grams of marrow, which can be taken from the bone used in a broth.
  5. Mix perfectly and fill the eggs, forming the egg again.
