Ingredients

  • 5 hard-boiled eggs
  • bechamel
  • butter
  • grated cheese

Directions

  1. Put half of the white sauce in a heat-resistant dish of the oven
  2. put the 5 hard-boiled eggs cut into slices on top.
  3. Top with the rest of the sauce
  4. Drizzle with butter and sprinkle with grated cheese.
  5. Bake and let it brown.
