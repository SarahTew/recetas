Ingredients

  • 1 pound of spaghetti
  • 1 pound of sliced ​​bacon
  • 2 cups of cooked corn
  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • 1-4 teaspoon of pepper
  • 1-4 pounds of grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Boil the spaghetti and drain.
  2. Grease a casserole dish and pour a quarter of the spaghetti into it.
  3. Add a third of the bacon, corn and cheese.
  4. Sprinkle with a little pepper and put part of the Butter. Repeat this until you have used all the ingredients.
  5. Bake at moderate heat, 350 degrees for 25 minutes.
