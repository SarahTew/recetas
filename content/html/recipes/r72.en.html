Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds potatoes
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 5 eggs
  • 1 pound butter

Directions

  1. Boil and mash the potatoes.
  2. Add the butter and eggs one by one and beat when adding.
  3. Fry in hot lard by tablespoons.
  4. Drain and serve in syrup or sprinkle with sugar.
