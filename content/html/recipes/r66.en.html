Ingredients

  • 2 pounds boiled sweet potato
  • 1-2 cup muscovado sugar
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 12 marshmallows

Directions

  1. Place in a greased pan with butter.
  2. Cover sweet potatoes (sweet potato) with sugar and butter.
  3. Cook in a moderate heat oven until the sugar melts and forms a caramel.
  4. If you want, place the "marshmallows" on the sweet potato.
  5. Put back on the top rack of the oven for a few minutes to let the marshmallows brown.
