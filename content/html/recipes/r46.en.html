Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet potato boiled and mashed
  • 1-2 cup sugar
  • 1-4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1-4 cup wheat flour
  • 1-4 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter melted
  • 2 beaten eggs
  • 1-4 teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. Mix the ingredients in the order they appear, beat to join them well.
  2. Pour into an engrated mold and cook in a moderate heat oven for 20 to 30 minutes.
