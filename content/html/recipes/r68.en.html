Ingredients

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/3 teaspoon vanilla essence
  • 6 clear
  • 3 teaspoons Royal powder 1 1/2 of dulce de leche or jam
  • MERINGUE
  • 5 egg whites
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1-4 teaspoon vanilla essence

Directions

  1. Beat the yolks with the sugar until they are foamy, add the essence and stop beating.
  2. Add the egg whites beaten to forming peaks gently stirring with wooden spoon.
  3. Sift together the flour and Royal Powder and add to the previous mixture stirring slowly, nothing more than to incorporate.
  4. Pour the batter into a buttered and floured mold and cook in at moderate heat for 30 minutes
  5. Once cooked remove from rack and allow to cool
  6. Cut the cake into three layers, then spread amply with the dulce de lechet.
  7. Reassemble the cake and decorated with meringue.
  8. Garnish with figs.
