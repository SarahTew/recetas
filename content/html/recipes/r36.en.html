• 6 glasses
  • 4 cups of hot water
  • 4 tablespoons barley powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 1-2 teaspoons juice lemon
  1. Mix the barley with a little cold water, add the hot water and boil for a few minutes, stirring constantly.
  2. Strain the barley, add the sugar and lemon juice.
  3. Serve very cold.
