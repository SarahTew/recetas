Ingredients

  • 1/2 can of condensed milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 tablespoons flour
  • /-4 teaspoon cinnamon powder
  • 1-4 teaspoon vanilla essence

Directions

  1. Dilute the condensed milk with the water, add the flour and heat on the stove until it thickens.
  2. Add the egg whites to the hot milk by tablespoons
  3. To the hot milk are added, by tablespoons, the already beatened egg whites, making sure to cover them so that the whites are cooked.
  4. Then remove them with the skimmer and place them in a dish
  5. Add the lightly beaten yolks to the hot milk and cook lightly then pour evertying over the egg whites.
