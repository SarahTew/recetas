Ingredients

  • 1 1-2 cups egg whites
  • 1-4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon of cream of tartar
  • 1 1-4 cups sugar
  • I teaspoon vanilla
  • 3-4 cups wheat flour
  • 4 tablespoons cocoa

Directions

  1. Beat the egg whites with a wire whisk until they form peaks, add the cream and continue beating until hard, but not dry.
  2. Add the sugar two tablespoons at a time.
  3. Stir from top to bottom and keep adding sugar by tablespoons until finished.
  4. While stirring add the vanilla, fold in the flour, salt and cocoa four times adding little by little while stirring.
  5. Pour into a bundt mold in the center and cook in a slow oven (325 F) for one hour.
