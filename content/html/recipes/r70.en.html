Ingredients

  • pork tenderloin
  • beef
  • bacon
  • salt
  • nutmeg
  • 1 raw egg
  • white bread crumbs
  • a glass of Sauterne wine
  • vegetables
  • pepper salt
  • a little broth

Directions

  1. Choose the loin and cut it down the middle and open it lengthwise and spread very carefully so that the meat is stretched.
  2. Grind beef, pork, bacon, salt and nutmeg, add 1 raw egg, white bread crumbs and a glass of Sauterne wine.
  3. Place the mixture on the loin, add slices of hard-boiled egg and ham to the filling
  4. Roll and tie the meat so that the filling does not come out.
  5. Surround delicately cut legumes, drizzle with white wine, salt, pepper and a little broth.
  6. When the loin is perfectly cooked in the oven, when it has color, the corresponding juice is passed and served very hot surrounding it with roasted eggplants.
  7. It can also be served cold with mayonnaise.
