Ingredients

  • 1 pound of cod
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 2 sliced onions
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup olives
  • 1 tablespoon capers
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 pound sliced potatoes
  • 2 roasted ripe peppers
  • 1/4 cup raisins

Directions

  1. Put the cod in hot water, remove the skin and bones and cut into medium-sized pieces.
  2. In a casserole dish, put pieces of cod, onions and potatoes, tomato sauce, olives, capers and raisins, garlic and strips of peppers.
  3. Pour the oil and cook slowly for 30 minutes.
  4. It can be baked in the oven.
