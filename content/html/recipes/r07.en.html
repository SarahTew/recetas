Ingredients

  • RECIPE1 1 chicken
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • cognac
  • a quarter pound of butter
  • RECIPE2 2 pounds of potatoes
  • water
  • salt to taste
  • half a pound of butter
  • pepper
  • grated nutmeg
  • 2 egg yolks
  • a quarter of milk cream
  • 6 tablespoons grated cheese

Directions

  1. RECIPE1 Clean the chicken, wash it and dry it, season it with salt, spray the cog√±ac and place it in the mold you are going to use for roasting.
  2. Smear the butter and put it in the oven at regular temperature
  3. RECIPE2 Boil the potatoes with salt, once they are soft, make them into a puree, add the butter, yolks, cream of milk and 4 tablespoons of grated cheese, season with pepper and nutmeg, putting them in a baking dish, sprinkle with the remaining one, sprinkle it with a little melted butter and put it in the hot oven to gratin.
  4. Once the chicken is ready, slice and serve with the puree.
