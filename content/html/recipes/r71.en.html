Ingredients

  • veal
  • salt
  • bread
  • cheese
  • egg
  • lemon
  • tomato sauce

Directions

  1. Clean the veal chops and season with salt, cover in white bread crumbs and some grated cheese, beaten egg and finally in bread crumbs mixed with the desired amount of cheese.
  2. Fry until well browned.
  3. Serve garnished with lemon and tomato sauce.
