Ingredients

  • Six onions
  • three tablespoons of butter
  • one liter of broth
  • three slices of bread
  • half a cup of grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Cut the onions into slices and fry them in butter, until golden brown.
  2. Add the broth is added and bring to a boil.
  3. Toast the slices of bread and place on top of the soup in a casserole dish, lightly covering them with the cheese.
  4. Finally, put the casserole in the oven for ten minutes, until cheese is melted.
